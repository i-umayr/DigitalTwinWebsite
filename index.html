About Us

DIIM Lab

The Lab of Design Innovation and Intelligent Manufacturing (DIIM) was established by Dr. Sheng Yang in September 2020, affiliated with the College of Engineering at the University of Guelph in Ontario, Canada.

We are a highly multidisciplinary research team with members from mechanical engineering, computer engineering, and data science. Our mission is to develop the next generation of intelligent engineering systems through synergistic research on AI, digital twins, and human-centered design.
